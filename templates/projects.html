Our Project

In this part, we will present our procedure in conducting the project step by step. But modeling part will be shown in Models page.

The first graph explores the total transaction revenues in every subcontinent worldwidely. The vertical axis represents the total transaction revenues which we want to predict. Three colors from light to deep blue in the barchart mean different device categories —— tablet, mobile, desktop. We found that destop device accounts for the most proportion. After the exloration, the graph told us the total transaction revenues in North America are the most.

Because North America takes the largest proportion, we plot the total transaction revenues in every state of USA. The deeper the color, the more transaction revenues. The top 3 states are New York, California and Texas.

In this graph, we took two features in traffic source —— trafficSource.source and trafficSource.medium, then integrated these two features to explore the relationship with total transaction revenues.
